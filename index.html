Event Description

Skilled in web development? Or even looking for venting and emancipation? "SUP'COM JUNIOR ENTREPRISE" is announcing its outstanding event "CODE IN THE DARK" for its 1st edition, which will take place on March 26, 2022, at the Technopole el Ghazala - Ariana Conference venue. "CODE IN THE DARK" is the perfect place for those who have a keen interest in programming and development so don't hesitate to join us and learn from each other. The event is free and open to everyone. It's the best place for you to spend a Saturday evening, and dance far into the night.
